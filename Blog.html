Venti: The Enigmatic Bard of Mondstadt

Venti is a playable Anemo character in Genshin Impact. He is a free-spirited, wine-loving bard of Mondstadt, belying his identity as Barbatos, the Anemo Archon.

Character Overview

Venti is known for his charming personality and musical talent. He plays the lyre and often entertains the people of Mondstadt with his songs. His carefree nature hides a deeper wisdom and connection to the world.

Abilities

  • Normal Attack: Performs up to 6 consecutive shots with a bow.
  • Elemental Skill: Summons a wind current that can lift players and enemies.
  • Elemental Burst: Calls upon the power of Anemo to create a storm that deals AoE damage.

Ascension Materials

To ascend Venti, players need specific materials, including:

  • Venti's Stella Fortuna
  • Various local specialties from Mondstadt

Constellations

Each Constellation activation requires one Venti's Stella Fortuna. Venti has been featured in several Event Wishes, enhancing his availability for players.

Conclusion

Venti is not just a bard; he is a symbol of freedom and the spirit of Mondstadt. His character adds depth to the Genshin Impact universe, making him a beloved choice among players.
