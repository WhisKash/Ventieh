I Love

How can I help but fall for you, when every glance feels like the sky’s new hue, and your laughter, soft as morning rain, eases my heart and dispels the pain? Your words, a melody so sweet, make time slow down and life complete. With every step, you draw me near, a quiet pull I hold so dear. It’s not the surface that calls me in, but the kindness and light that live within. Your soul, a spark that gently glows, a love that blooms and softly grows. I fall for you, not with a rush, but with every moment, every touch, for in you, I find a peace untold, a love so warm, it’s worth the hold.
